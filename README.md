# Serbian A2–B1 vocabulary deck (Serbian ↔ Russian) for Anki
I hope you'll find this deck useful for learning Serbian.

I created it and continue to expand it while studying Serbian with a teacher. My approach is based on the idea that memorizing isolated words is more difficult and less effective than learning them through their use in everyday speech. That's why many cards include example sentences to provide context and make vocabulary easier to remember.

If you notice any mistakes or have suggestions for improvements or additional vocabulary, please feel free to contact me or open an issue. Your feedback is always welcome.
